About SB Global Ventures

Empowering cross-border trade and sustainable products from India to the world.

Who We Are

SB Global Ventures India is a dynamic export house dedicated to promoting India’s heritage of craftsmanship and sustainable products. As a government-certified export company with extensive knowledge of global trade and product sourcing, we have built a reputation for reliability, transparency, and excellence in international markets. Our expertise in export processes, compliance, and buyer requirements ensures a smooth and professional experience for our global partners.

We specialize in Handicrafts, Eco Products, Leather Goods, and premium Indian Tea & Coffee, offering carefully curated collections that combine tradition, quality, and contemporary appeal. Every product we export reflects our values of eco-consciousness, fair trade, and sustainability, ensuring that buyers not only receive premium goods but also support ethical and responsible practices.

Our Mission

To empower Indian artisans, growers, and producers by taking their creations to global markets while ensuring international buyers receive premium, eco-conscious, and ethically sourced products with unmatched service. Our mission is to be a trusted bridge between India’s rich heritage and global demand, creating long-term, value-driven partnerships.

Why Choose Us?

  • Government-certified export company with proven credibility
  • Extensive knowledge of export and product sourcing with a strong focus on quality and compliance
  • Wide product portfolio: handicrafts, eco products, leather goods, tea & coffee
  • Vast supply network of verified artisans, growers, and manufacturers
  • Quality assurance & export compliance at every stage
  • Reliable logistics support for timely, hassle-free delivery
  • Commitment to sustainability & eco-friendly sourcing practices that reflect our core values
