About Us

SB Global Ventures is a multi-category export enterprise rooted in India. We specialize in sourcing and exporting top-quality Indian goods, starting with eco-conscious handcrafted home décor through our flagship brand Sack & Soul. Our future expansions include lifestyle products, consumer electronics, and private label solutions under diverse brand identities. We aim to be a one-stop global partner for reliable, scalable, and culturally-rich exports.
