About Us

SB Global Ventures is a multi-category export company offering global buyers access to high-quality goods. Our mission is to become a trusted name in international trade across segments including home decor, consumer electronics, wellness, and private label goods. While our operations originate in South Asia, our brand maintains a global identity committed to quality, sustainability, and long-term partnerships. Our flagship brand Sack & Soul delivers premium handcrafted products with a story, while new verticals will soon cater to diverse international markets.
