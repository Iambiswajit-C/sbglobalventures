Partnering Globally with Purpose

We connect traditional craftsmanship with global markets, focusing on eco-conscious, ethically-sourced handcrafted goods.

Who We Are

SB Global Ventures is a B2B export initiative based in India, dedicated to sourcing sustainable, artisan-made products including baskets, home décor, and textile-based crafts. We ensure ethical sourcing, reliable delivery, and consistent quality.

What We Offer

Our range includes water hyacinth baskets, jute décor, cotton textiles, and custom-designed handmade goods. We work closely with cooperatives and NGOs to ensure authenticity and scalability.

Social & Environmental Impact

We empower rural artisans, especially women, by giving their crafts a global platform. Our supply chain reduces waste and encourages eco-friendly materials.
