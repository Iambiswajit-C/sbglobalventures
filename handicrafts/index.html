Jute Products

Our jute handicrafts include rugs, baskets, and eco-friendly bags. Known as the "golden fiber," jute is completely biodegradable and one of the most popular sustainable materials. Our jute products combine durability with traditional Indian weaving techniques, making them ideal for export markets seeking eco-conscious products.
